Honor code policy

Last modified: November 25, 2022 (view archived versions)

Introduction

Climaxanswers is devoted to learners and their learning results. As we know, academic integrity is crucial in learning, and we strive hard to maintain it.

By using our Services, you are agreeing to honor code. Please read them carefully.

Using our services

We offer services that enhance learning but do not replace it. We offer online learning support 24/7. We provide step-by-step explanations to assist students anytime they need it, anywhere.

Our services should be used to aid learning, help in mastering coursework, and help you prepare for a test.

Our services are strictly not to be used for fraud or cheating, for example, asking for answers to an ongoing exam or a test or copying solutions found on the internet and submitting them as your work. These activities beat learning aims, and this is not just for anybody.

We don't tolerate any abuse of our services from our platform. Any misuse can lead to severe consequences, such as being blocked from the platform. Any other actions may be taken to preserve the integrity of the platform and company. This may involve pulling down any material that is offending and closing the account of someone involved in misusing our services and our platform.

Use of any services or information offered on our platforms, such as materials, solutions, answers and information to cheat on an exam or graded assignment, will automatically be described as a violation of our honour code, and strict action shall be taken.
